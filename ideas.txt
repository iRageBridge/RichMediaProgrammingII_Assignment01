XML examples:

Weather: https://openweathermap.org/api
Currency: https://currencylayer.com/
Population: http://api.population.io/

Implementation:

Weather: Series of shapes being blown in the direction the local wind is blowing.
Currency: Have to direct falling coins into a moneybox with a certain currency, the exhange rate is displayed when you do.
Population: Throw a dart at an atlas, wherever it lands tells you population information on that country.
